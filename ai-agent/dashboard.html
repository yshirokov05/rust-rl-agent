INTERPRETATION GUIDE

Episode Reward Mean

The "Score". It represents the average points the bot earned in its last 100 lives. If this goes up, the bot is learning to survive and gather.

Approx KL

Learning Velocity. How much the "brain" is changing. Ideally between 0.001 and 0.03. Too high = erratic learning; Too low = stuck.

Policy/Value Loss

Neural Confusion. Lower is generally better, but "stable" is more important. It measures how much the bot's expectations match reality.

FPS (Frames Per Second)

World Speed. The bot thinks at 10 ticks per second. Anything below 10 means the system is lagging.

Entropy Loss

Creativity/Curiosity. Measures how much the bot is experimenting. It usually decreases over time as the bot becomes an "expert".
